The Economy Wars

v0.0.2 Aeturnum

Within an intricately constructed alternate world, players find themselves in a world with rising tensions, teetering on the brink of an impending war. From negotiating trade pacts to espionage and resource allocation, The Economy Wars challenges participants to utilize their diplomatic, economic, and strategic skills to shape the course of history in a dynamically evolving fictional universe.
